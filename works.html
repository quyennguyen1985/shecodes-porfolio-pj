Ong Sang Tao club

Ong Sang Tao science club is the most long-standing club of Nguyen Thi Minh Khai high school based in Ho Chi Minh city, Vietnam.


This collection below is designed specifically for Nguyen Thi Minh Khai high school's students.
These designs are for making badges and for internal sales purposes


THAP DONG HO MINH KHAI

(Minh Khai's clock tower)

As one of the most symbolic objects, I decided to potray the iconic clock tower located at the front of our school.

MIKI FC

(Minh Khai football club)

Designed to show gratitude towards our school's football club as they have competed well and won in many important games

NAM SINH MINH KHAI

(Minh Khai's "meow" students)

Our school has also been home to a couple of stray cats, one of them is a male so I turned him into our male student: wears a cool uniform and usually is a gentlemeow!

NU SINH MINH KHAI

(Minh Khai's fe"meow" students)

This gorgeous cat is the other one in the feline duo. Same with the boy, she wears our iconic female uniform in purple. She is honey sweet but will bite!

HO BOI MINH KHAI

(Minh Khai's pool)

The pool is one of the best places to take some mesmerizing pictures at our school. And we don't raise ducks, just so you know.
