My story

This gorgeous girl is my senior. Her name is Hà Phương Uyên and we got to know each other by working on the same project in the same department.

She is so calm, friendly yet one of the nicest people I have ever met.

Once the design concept was pixel art, I was sent not only a free tool but also a whole document of of instructions written by her, including shortcuts and tips for beginners.

I was surprised and not to mention emotional!

That is when I first interact with pixel art and I got used to it quite fast fortunately. Thanks to her, I'm in love with it now!

My progress

When I first heard about Aseprite, I have no idea that it is an extremely popular tool for pixel artists and artists in general.

I neither have a tablet for hand-drawing nor a computer mouse, so it really is a challenge for me whenever I have to do a sketch.

As you can see, there is a white layer underneath the pixel cat and that layer is a normal sketch from iPad.

With the uploaded sketch, it is way easier for me to finish the lineart and go on coloring it.

At first, I wasn't used to using the shortcuts but by working on projects from time to time, I should say I have quite mastered it now!
